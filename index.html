About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist with expertise in developing AI solutions for real-world problems. My background spans computer science and statistics, with a focus on deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.


I specialize in building robust machine learning systems that translate complex data into actionable insights. With experience in both research and production environments, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI techniques and practical business applications.


My research interests include reinforcement learning, generative models, and multimodal learning systems. I'm particularly fascinated by how we can create more efficient and interpretable AI that solves challenging problems across domains.


When I'm not training models or analyzing data, you can find me exploring open-source projects, contributing to the AI community, and staying current with the rapidly evolving field of machine learning.
